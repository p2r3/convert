FAQ

Is JPG to PNG conversion lossless?

The PNG file itself is lossless, but any detail already lost in the original JPG cannot be restored.

When should I use PNG after converting from JPG?

Use PNG when you plan further edits, annotations, or repeated exports where extra JPG recompression would hurt clarity.
